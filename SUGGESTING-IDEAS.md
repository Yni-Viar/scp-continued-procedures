## About adding an SCP to the game?
An SCP should meet this criteria:
1. SCP should be lesser than 1000 (this restriction will be off in 2026-01-01, when SCP-2028 will be added)
2. SCP should not contain NSFW elements.
3. SCP should be not so popular (example - SCP-096) (this restriction will be off after 5.0.0 for mid-popular SCPs (such as SCP-323, SCP-009, SCP-178), 2026-07-01 for very popular SCPs (such as SCP-049, SCP-939, SCP-008) )

**Also p.3 does not apply on game's Author**
